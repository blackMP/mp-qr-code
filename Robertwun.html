LIASE’s new inspiration for SS19 is Avant Gard French-Russian creative Aleksandra Ekster, cubo-futurist artist and co-founder of Art Deco. The correlation of colors, forms, and lines is what strikes us and what we bring in this new collection. Once again modern, geometrical shapes fused with colorful stones. Each Piece is handmade in Florence in a small laboratory using traditional jewellery techniques. The pieces are in 925% sterling Silver and 18k gold plated mixed with hard and precious stones such as amethysts, lapislazzuli, agate, sapphires. For this summer collection we will be alternating colorful stones geometrically cut with enamel painting to give a fresh and feminine look to the jewellery.
