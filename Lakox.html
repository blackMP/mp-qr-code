ACCESSORIES

OKHTEIN, Arabic for “two sisters”, is an Egyptian higher contemporary bags and accessories brand, focusing on artisanal craftsmanship coupled with quirky unusual shapes and stories. Laureates of the OpenMyMed Prize and Vogue Talents Italy-Arabia Accessory Fashion Prize winners, OKHTEIN has fast become a certified, must-have accessories brand. The label has been garnering strong recognition as featured on CNN, Vogue Italy-Arabia, Harper’s Bazaar UK-Arabia and Love Magazine, whilst continuously stacking up support and endorsements from Beyonce, Emma Watson, Emma Roberts, Anna Wintour, Gigi Hadid and Sara Sampaio, to name just a few.
